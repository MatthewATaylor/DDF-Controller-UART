1 - Solid color
	q - red
	w - orange
	e - yellow
	r - green
	t - blue
	y - purple
	u - bright white
	i - warm white
2 - Wave
	q - vertical wave
	w - horizontal wave

Arrow up - increase brightness
Arrow down - decrease brightness
Shift - switch between background and interactive mode
	Background - looping animation
	Interactive - trigger with spacebar
